<objective>
Fix AS5600 feed distance accumulation in firmware and restore broken test fixture.

Purpose: Feed distance currently only updates on STATUS polls (~500ms), missing fast rotation and producing wrong deltas across the 0/4095 encoder boundary. Moving accumulation to every main-loop tick with wrap-around correction and magnet-offline guarding ensures accurate, continuous tracking. The MockSerial fixture fix restores 54 broken tests as a baseline for new Phase 6 tests.

Output: Modified uart_protocol.cpp with continuous accumulation; MockSerial accepting zero-arg construction.
</objective>
